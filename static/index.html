QUANT FORECAST MODEL

Probabilities & scorelines for every World Cup match.

A time-weighted Dixon-Coles bivariate-Poisson model fit on 49,453 international results from 1872 to 2026 — win/draw/loss odds, exact-score distributions and expected goals for any fixture. The model runs entirely in your browser.

MODEL & DATA

Forecasts come from a time-weighted Dixon-Coles bivariate-Poisson model. Data: martj42/international_results (1872–2026). Injuries, red cards, and squad changes are not modeled; group letters are reconstructed from played fixtures.
